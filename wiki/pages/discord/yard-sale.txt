====#yard-sale====

In an effort to make FS/FT posts more visible and nice... we ask that you please start to use this template.

<code>
**Item Name:** Item Name, Including Model Numbers or Versions
**Item Condition:** New/Like New/Refurbished/Used/Not Working/Untested
**Item Images:** Your Imgur Album Link Goes Here
**Item Price:** Item Price in USD
**Item Shipping:** Free Shipping/Calculated Shipping (Based on ZIP)/Local Pickup | Carrier & Box Size/Weight | Signature Options
**Item Location:** Put your state code here, such as NY, CA, TX, FL, AK
**Other Notes:** Anything of note about the item should go here.
</code>
